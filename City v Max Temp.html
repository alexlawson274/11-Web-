Comparing City Latitude and Max Temperture

The graph above shows the relationship between the latitude of cities in our data set and the Maximum temperature. As we can observe from the visual relationship, cities that are south of the equator (below 0) have a high maximum that is consistent across all cities in our dataset Cities that are north of the equator (above 0) their maximum temperatures drop the further north they are. There is a negative correlation between latitude and Max temp in Northern Hemisphere.
