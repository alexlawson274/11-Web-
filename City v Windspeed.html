Comparing City Latitude and Wind Speed

The graph above shows the relationship between the latitude of cities in our data set and the Windspeed. Whilst there is no direct correlation between latitude and wind speed we can see that in the extreme north, there are some outliers with very high wind speeds. This may be due to being in a sub artic region, where high wind speeds would be expected.
